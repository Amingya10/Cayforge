Getting Started — 4 Steps
Create your account
Click Sign In on the main page and create a free account. Choose your currency — Nigerian Naira (₦) or US Dollar ($) — this sets your payment method if you upgrade later.
Tip: Your free account gives you 5 designs per month. No credit card needed.
Get your Anthropic API key
Go to console.anthropic.com, sign up, and add at least $5 credit. Copy your API key (starts with sk-ant-...) and paste it into the Anthropic API Key field in the Studio.
Tip: Each design generation costs less than $0.01. $5 gives you hundreds of designs. On paid Clayforge plans, this is bundled — no key needed.
Describe your ceramic piece
In the Design Studio, type a description of the piece you want to create. Be as specific or as vague as you like. Choose a form type and style inspiration from the dropdowns.
Example: "A tall vase inspired by Hausa architecture, with geometric relief patterns and a deep indigo glaze that fades to warm terracotta at the base"
Generate, save and export
Click "Fire the kiln — Generate Design". Your full design brief appears in seconds. Save it to your Library, export it as a PDF, or copy the text to share with clients or your studio team.
Tip: Use the Ceramic Toolkit for glaze recipes, kiln scheduling, cost calculation and order management.

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need an API key to use Clayforge? ▾
On the free plan, you need your own Anthropic API key (from console.anthropic.com). It costs less than $0.01 per design. On paid plans (Stoneware and Porcelain), API access is bundled — no key needed.
Is my design data private? ▾
Yes. Your designs belong entirely to you. We never share, sell or use your designs for training. Designs saved to your library are only accessible to you when logged in.
How do I pay for a subscription? ▾
Nigerian users pay via Paystack — supporting card, bank transfer and USSD. International users pay via Stripe — all major cards accepted. You choose your currency when you sign up.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▾
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account page. You keep access until the end of your billing period and your designs are always exportable as PDFs.
Does the toolkit data save between sessions? ▾
Yes. Glaze recipes, kiln logs, clay recipes and orders are all saved in your browser's local storage. They persist between sessions on the same device and browser.
Can I use Clayforge on my phone? ▾
Yes. Clayforge is fully responsive and works on all devices — phone, tablet, and desktop. All features including the toolkit work on mobile.
How accurate are the technical specifications? ▾
The AI generates industry-standard specifications based on established ceramic practices. However, always test glazes and clay bodies in your own kiln before full production — every kiln behaves differently.
Can I share my designs with clients? ▾
Yes. Export any design as a PDF and share it with clients, collaborators or your studio team. The PDF includes all design sections and is formatted professionally with the Clayforge branding.
I'm getting "Failed to fetch" — what do I do? ▾
This usually means your internet connection dropped temporarily. Try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, email us at hello@clayforge.app and we'll help you right away.
